Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use the app?
No. The app works fully on-device. Signing in is optional and only used to sync your practice settings and session history across your own devices.

How do I enable practice reminders?
Turn on reminders in the app's settings. Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device — nothing is sent from a server.

How do I delete my data?
Uninstalling the app removes all on-device data. To remove synced data, email us at the address above and we'll delete your account's synced settings, session history, and stats.
